Our Story

Crafting luxury living experiences through exceptional design and timeless furniture

Founded in Excellence

Slomandaz was born from a vision to bring international luxury furniture standards to Nigerian homes. Our journey began in Port Harcourt with a simple belief: every space deserves to tell a story of sophistication, comfort, and personal style.

We curate each piece with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that our furniture not only meets the highest standards of quality but also reflects the unique character of modern African living. From contemporary minimalism to timeless classics, our collection speaks to those who appreciate the finer things in life.

Today, Slomandaz stands as a beacon of luxury living, serving discerning customers across Nigeria who refuse to compromise on quality, design, or comfort.

OUR VALUES

Material Quality

We source only the finest materials from trusted suppliers, ensuring every piece meets our exacting standards for durability and beauty.

Craftsmanship

Each piece is crafted by skilled artisans who bring decades of experience and passion to their work, resulting in furniture that lasts generations.

Design Innovation

We stay ahead of global design trends while respecting timeless principles, creating furniture that is both contemporary and enduring.

"Our mission is simple: to transform houses into homes through furniture that speaks to the soul. Every piece we select tells a story of quality, beauty, and the Nigerian spirit of excellence."
Kelechi Chilaka Founder & Creative Director
